Barlinale is an independent counter film festival taking place February 18–20 at Tennis Bar in Berlin, Germany. With a focus on non-conventional and non-fiction cinema, the three-day event seeks to foster cine-scenes in championing screening as practice, wherein participating filmmakers to extend the invitation in selecting a pairing film.

We believe that politics are to be practised, not simply programmed, and seek to promote financial equity and transparency between cultural workers and filmmakers alike. Revenue is split 50/50 between the participants and the festival until its minimal operating budget (€200) is recuperated, whereupon all additional proceeds are given to the filmmakers. However modest a screening fee, it's more than our leading competitors.

The hope is to develop a format that can be replicated to nurture a truly independent cinema, asking: What if the money we spent on submission fees went into screening our work instead?

TICKETS

10-20€ sliding scale festival pass at the door, granting access to all events.

LOCATION

Tennis Bar is located at Reuterstr 95, Neukölln, Germany. The screening room is located in the basement, which is sadly not wheelchair accessible.
